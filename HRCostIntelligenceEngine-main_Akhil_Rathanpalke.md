## Project Description

HR Cost Intelligence Engine is an AI-powered solution that helps organizations understand the actual cost of meetings and employee time spent on projects. The platform connects employee data, meeting schedules, and project assignments to calculate project-wise HR expenditure in real time.

The system is designed for HR teams, finance departments, and project managers who need visibility into workforce utilization and budget allocation. By automatically attributing meeting hours to projects, the platform helps organizations make better financial and operational decisions.

---

## Approach

To solve the problem, I designed a system that links employee salaries, calendar events, and project assignments into a single analytics dashboard.

### How I approached the problem

* Analyzed how organizations struggle to track meeting-related HR costs.
* Designed a workflow to map employee meeting participation to project budgets.
* Built dashboards to visualize project expenditure and workforce utilization.
* Used AI concepts to automate project attribution and cost calculations.
* Focused on providing real-time insights for HR and finance teams.

### User Flow

1. Add employee details and salary information.
2. Create or import meeting records.
3. Assign meetings to projects.
4. Calculate employee costs based on meeting duration.
5. View project-wise and employee-wise cost analytics.
